JOJO´S TAROTS CARDS STANDS

0

The Fool

The Fool (ザ・フール（愚者） Za Fūru) is the Stand of Iggy. The Fool does not have an actual psychic manifestation (i.e. it is not normally visible), but instead takes its form by manipulating sand and dust particles in the environment (and as such, it is one of the few Stands truly visible to everyone, not only to Stand users).

I

The Magician

Magician's Red (マジシャンズレッド（魔術師の赤） Majishanzu Reddo) is the Stand of Muhammad Avdol. Magician's Red is an extremely powerful close-range Stand that while limited in versatility, more than makes up for it with its devastatingly powerful arsenal of capabilities specifically developed for combat.

II

The High Prienstess

High Priestess (ハイプリエステス（女教皇） Hai Puriesutesu) is the Stand of Midler. High Priestess is a Stand bound to minerals. Its "default" form is that of a small creature having a tribal mask with vicious eyes and bared fangs for a face, and clawed thin, muscular arms sprouting out a mush of inorganic matter.

III

The Empress

Empress (エンプレス（女帝） Enpuresu) is the Stand of Nena. Empress is a Stand bound to its own user's blood, first appearing as a small drop of blood from Nena. Once the blood drop hits a person, the true Stand is revealed.

IV

The Emperor

Emperor (エンペラー（皇帝） Enperā) is the Stand of Hol Horse. Emperor is a Stand in the form of an elaborate, impractical-looking revolver with some futuristic elements.

V

The High Erophant

Hierophant Green (ハイエロファントグリーン（法皇の緑） Haierofanto Gurīn) is the Stand of Noriaki Kakyoin. Hierophant Green is an elastic and remote Stand, capable of being deployed far away from its user and performing actions from a distance. Kakyoin has possessed this Stand since birth.

VI

The Lovers

Lovers (ラバーズ（恋人） Rabāzu), is the Stand of Steely Dan. Lovers is, unlike most Stands, of microscopic size, enabling it to infiltrate the human body unnoticed. It resembles a robotic humanoid creature with an insectoid head and with crab pincers for hands.

VII

The Chariot

Silver Chariot (シルバーチャリオッツ（銀の戦車） Shirubā Chariottsu) is the Stand of Jean Pierre Polnareff. Silver Chariot appears as a thin, robotic humanoid clad in silver, medieval armor, armed with a basket-hilted foil.

VIII

Strength

Strength (ストレングス（力） Sutorengusu) is the Stand of Forever. Inspired by the famous merchant brigantine, Mary CelesteW,[1] Strength is a Stand bound to a small yacht, which is then transformed into a gigantic derelict freighter named "Big Daddy".

IX

The Hermit

Hermit Purple (ハーミットパープル（隠者の紫） Hāmitto Pāpuru) is the Stand of Joseph Joestar. Hermit Purple manifests itself as multiple, purple, thorn-covered vines that spawn from Joseph's hands.The vines are strong enough to support his own weight and long enough to bind someone completely.

X

Wheel of Fortune

Wheel of Fortune (ホウィール・オブ・フォーチュン（運命の車輪） Howīru Obu Fōchun) is the Stand of ZZ. Wheel of Fortune is bound to a car and, like Strength, is also visible to non-Stand users. Its appearance can change wildly because ZZ can change the features of the car to suit his purposes.

XI

Justice

Justice (ジャスティス（正義） Jasutisu) is the Stand of Enya the Hag. Justice primarily takes the appearance of a massive cloud of fog, being large enough to envelop a small cemetery. When materialized, it appears as a skeletal phantom wearing a large crown decorated with jeweled arches and a spike on top.

XII

The Hanged Man

Hanged Man (ハングドマン（吊られた男） Hangudo Man) is the Stand of J. Geil. Hanged Man's design reflects that of a mummy with mechanical elements, making it seem less human.[1] Its body is covered in bandages, with half its brain exposed, along with having two right hands as its master does.

XIII

Death

Death Thirteen (デスサーティーン（死神13） Desu Sātīn), sometimes stylized as "DEATH 13", is the Stand of Mannish Boy. Death Thirteen is unique in that it can only exist inside a dream world and thus requires that its victims are asleep. It is incredibly dangerous because it is certain to take people by surprise.

XIV

Temperance

Yellow Temperance (イエローテンパランス（黄の節制） Ierō Tenparansu) is the Stand of Rubber Soul. Yellow Temperance's blob-like nature and ability to absorb organic matter from physical contact result in one of the few Stands of the series that can be said to be physically invincible.

XV

The Devil

Ebony Devil (エボニーデビル（悪魔） Ebonī Debiru) is the Stand of Devo. Ebony Devil is risky in that Devo must be injured by his targets first in order for the Stand to work. However, when set up, it can then ambush its victim and proves to be elusive.

XVI

The Tower

Tower of Gray (タワー・オブ・グレー（灰の塔） Tawā Obu Gurē) is the Stand of Gray Fly. Tower of Gray is a small and fragile Stand. However, it is also incredibly fast and nimble, and its secondary jaw grants it unsuspected destructive power.

XVII

The Star

Star Platinum (スタープラチナ（星の白金） Sutā Purachina) is the Stand of Jotaro Kujo. Star Platinum is a close-range Stand, with a basic reach of only 2 meters from Jotaro's body, but with incredible strength, speed, and precision.

XVIII

The Moon

Dark Blue Moon (ダークブルームーン（暗青の月） Dāku Burū Mūn) is the Stand of Impostor Captain Tennille. Dark Blue Moon displays little to no personality by itself. It represents the Tarot Card The MoonW, which symbolizes trouble in water, lies, betrayal, and fear of the unknown.

XIX

The Sun

Sun (サン（太陽） San), is the Stand of Arabia Fats. Sun is an amazingly powerful Stand with a large range of action. However, it doesn't do anything to protect its user, who must stay nearby and hide himself.

XX

Judgement

Judgement (ジャッジメント（審判） Jajjimento) is the Stand of Cameo. Judgement is a fairly powerful Stand, having above average strength and speed as far as Stands go and is able to clash with Silver Chariot and come out without a scratch.

XXI

The World

The World (ザ・ワールド（世界） Za Wārudo) is the Stand of DIO. The World is an exceptionally powerful Close-range Stand much like Star Platinum as noted by Noriaki Kakyoin and Jotaro Kujo, but boasting an effective manifestation range of approximately 10 meters.
